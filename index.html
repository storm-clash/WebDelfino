First solo exhibition at the Cuban Art Factory (FAC).

After my first solo exhibition, I feel an overwhelming mix of pride, gratitude, and inspiration. This journey has been a deeply personal experience, reflecting countless hours of dedication and passion. Sharing my vision with others and seeing them connect with my work has been incredibly rewarding. It feels like a dream to have my art displayed for the world to see, and I am filled with gratitude for everyone who supported me along the way. This exhibition is not just a milestone, but also a reminder of why I fell in love with photography—to capture the world as I see it and to share that perspective with others.
